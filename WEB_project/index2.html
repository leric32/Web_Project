Enjoy the opportunities that SLAV Co. Asylum offers you

SLAV Co. Azylum, based in the vicinity of Belgrade, provides temporary protection to innocent animals that were abandoned for some reason and ended up on the street. With more than 20 years of experience, we have a large number of satisfied clients behind us, who have found new family members with us.

Look at the pets

If you have always wanted to have a pet, the cheapest and most humane way is to adopt it from an asylum.

Take note

Note which pets you like so you can see them right away when you come to SLAV Co. Asylum.

Come and choose

When you come to us, choose one or more pets that took your heart first and take them home right away.

Finished job

After these 3 steps, with a little paperwork, you get a new best and most loyal friend next to you.
